Fresh From Sea
How does it work?

Yes, indeed! We provide it fresh, never frozen, but you can freeze it right away for later use in the same packing as soon as you receive it.

The fish we buy are premium hook catch fish that are packaged and sent overnight. Through its flight, the fish is kept between 0°C and 4°C to preserve its freshness. Absence of chemicals or preservatives.

All of our fish are wild, and while we have a large variety of fish that are captured in the sea (salt water), we also have a small variety of freshwater fish, such as rohu and murrel. We don't receive any fish raised on farms.

Our in-house team of fish experts consists of highly skilled, seasoned professionals who maintain strong relationships with local fishermen. First-class hook-caught fish, which are typically highly expensive in the market, are what we purchase. In order to ensure the highest quality for you, the fish we purchase is caught overnight in the sea, then promptly chopped, cleaned, and packed before being transported the next day.
