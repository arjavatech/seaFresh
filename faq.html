Fresh From Sea
How does it work?

We have our in-house team of fish experts who are highly trained, experienced and well connected with local fisher men. The fish we buy are first quality hook catch fish that are usually priced high in the market. To give you the best quality, the fish we buy are caught over night from the sea, cut, cleaned and packed the same day and shipped the following night.

At Seafresh, we supply only fresh fish which are never frozen. We focus primarily on Indian varieties of fish. Our fish are cut, cleaned and Pan ready for our fish lovers to cook easily and spend their valuable time on other activities. We provide the option of Doorstep delivery as well Pick up options in stores.

The fish we purchase are first quality hook catch fish which are packed and shipped overnight. To maintain the freshness of the fish, it is maintained in 0°C to 4°C through their air travel. No Preservative or chemical added.

All our fish are wild caught, we have many varieties of fish that are caught in sea (Salt water), and we also have few varieties which are Fresh water fish, like Rohu, Murrel etc. We do not get any farm raised fish.
